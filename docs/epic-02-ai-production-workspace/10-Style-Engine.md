Status: Active

---

## Purpose

Define how style appears in the workspace.

---

## MVP Style Selection

Initial styles:

- Talking Head
- Podcast
- Education
- Business

---

## Style Controls

Each style should guide:

- Subtitle
- Motion
- Zoom
- B-roll
- Music
- Sound FX
- CTA
- Hook tone

---

## Workspace Behavior

User chooses a style during Upload.

Review shows which style was applied and what edit plan AI used.

Regeneration can reuse the same style or apply a different style later.

---

## Non-goals

- User-created style editor in first implementation
- Full style marketplace
- Complex animation builder
