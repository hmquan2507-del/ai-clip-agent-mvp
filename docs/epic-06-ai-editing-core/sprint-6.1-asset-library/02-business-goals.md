# Business Goals

Status: Draft

---

# Mission

Asset Library is a long-term strategic asset of AI Clip Agent.

Its primary objective is to reduce operational cost while increasing editing quality.

---

# Business Objectives

## Reduce AI Cost

Instead of asking an LLM to generate every editing decision from scratch, the system should reuse high-quality assets.

Expected result:

- Lower token consumption
- Lower inference cost
- Faster processing

---

## Increase Editing Quality

Professional editors reuse proven editing patterns.

The platform should follow the same philosophy.

Reusable assets produce more consistent outputs than fully generated content.

---

## Build Competitive Advantage

LLMs are accessible to everyone.

A proprietary Asset Library is unique to AI Clip Agent.

Over time, this library becomes intellectual property that competitors cannot easily replicate.

---

## Faster Rendering

Pre-indexed assets reduce the time required for editing and rendering.

---

## Improve User Experience

Users receive consistent, visually appealing videos with minimal manual adjustment.

---

# Key Performance Indicators

- Average editing time
- Asset reuse rate
- AI token consumption per production
- Average render duration
- User satisfaction
- Export success rate

---

# Long-term Vision

The Asset Library should evolve into one of the platform's most valuable assets.

As the library expands and usage data accumulates, AI Clip Agent will become increasingly efficient, reducing dependence on external AI models while improving editing quality.
