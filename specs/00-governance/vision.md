# Vision Statement

Build the world's most intelligent AI Video Production Platform that enables anyone to transform long-form content into high-quality short-form videos with minimal human effort.

---

# Mission

Help creators, businesses, agencies and educators save 80–95% of their video editing time by allowing AI to perform the entire production pipeline automatically.

Users should spend their time creating ideas, not editing timelines.

---

# Long-term Vision

AI Clip Agent will become an AI Video Production Operating System.

Instead of editing videos manually, users will manage AI productions.

Every production will be generated, improved, reviewed and exported by intelligent AI agents.

# Product Philosophy

AI should replace repetitive work.

Humans should focus on creativity.

Every feature must reduce manual editing.

Every screen must save time.

Automation is preferred over configuration.

Review is preferred over editing.
