---

## Purpose

Define early pricing direction for AI Clip Agent.

Pricing should reflect the core value: saving video editing time.

---

## Pricing Principle

Users are not buying AI calls.

Users are buying faster video production.

Pricing should connect to:

- Productions
- Generated clips
- Render minutes
- Storage
- Team usage
- Automation

---

## Suggested Pricing Model

Use subscription plans with monthly credits.

Credits can be consumed by:

- AI analysis
- Transcript generation
- Clip generation
- Style application
- Render/export
- Storage beyond included limits

---

## Plan Direction

### Free / Trial

Goal: let users experience the core value.

Possible limits:

- Small number of productions
- Watermarked exports
- Limited render minutes
- Limited styles

### Creator

For solo creators and personal brands.

Includes:

- More monthly credits
- No watermark
- Core styles
- Standard render queue

### Pro

For serious creators, marketers, and small businesses.

Includes:

- Higher credits
- More styles
- Faster render queue
- Saved brand settings
- Better export options

### Agency

For agencies and teams.

Includes:

- Multiple workspaces
- Team members
- Client organization
- Higher render limits
- Batch production
- Priority queue

---

## Pricing Metrics To Track

- Average credits per production
- Average render cost per export
- Average AI cost per production
- User willingness to pay per saved hour
- Conversion rate by persona
- Retention by production volume

---

## MVP Pricing Decision

Do not implement billing before the core production workflow is proven.

MVP should prepare the data model for credits and usage tracking, but billing can wait until the production workflow is valuable.

---

## Non-goals

- Complex enterprise pricing
- Marketplace pricing
- Social publishing revenue share
- Per-seat only pricing without usage limits
