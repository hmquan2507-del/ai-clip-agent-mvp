## Purpose

Define how AI Clip Agent measures product success.

The primary success metric is time saved in video production.

---

## North Star Metric

### Production Time Saved

Target: users save 80-95% of video editing time.

Example:

```text
Manual edit time: 3 hours
AI Clip Agent time: 10-30 minutes
Time saved: 83-94%
```

---

## Core Product Metrics

### Production Completion Rate

Percentage of started productions that reach export.

High completion means the product flow is clear and AI output is useful.

### Time To First AI Output

Time from upload complete to first generated clip ready for review.

This should be short enough that the user feels progress quickly.

### Time To Export

Time from upload to final exported clip.

This is the clearest measure of saved editing time.

### Approval Rate

Percentage of generated clips approved by users.

Low approval rate means highlight detection, style engine, or prompt quality needs work.

### Regeneration Rate

Percentage of clips requiring regeneration.

Some regeneration is healthy. Too much regeneration means AI output is not reliable.

### Manual Edit Rate

Percentage of clips where users manually change hook, subtitle, caption, CTA, B-roll, or music.

The goal is to reduce manual edits over time.

## AI Quality Metrics

- Highlight selection quality
- Hook quality
- Subtitle accuracy
- Style consistency
- CTA relevance
- B-roll relevance
- Music fit
- Render quality

---

## Business Metrics

- Productions created per user
- Clips exported per user
- Credits consumed per production
- Render cost per export
- Conversion from free to paid
- Subscription retention

---

## MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is successful when:

- User can upload a video.
- AI can generate multiple clip suggestions.
- User can review and approve clips.
- System can render/export clips.
- User saves meaningful editing time compared with manual editing.

---

## Metrics Not Prioritized In MVP

- Advanced team analytics
- Multi-organization reporting
- Social publishing analytics
- Creator revenue analytics

These belong to later SaaS and analytics epics.
