## Core Value Proposition

AI Clip Agent helps creators transform raw long-form videos into edited short-form videos automatically.

Users upload once, AI edits everything, and the user only reviews and exports.

---

## Primary Promise

Save 80–95% of video editing time.

---

## Before AI Clip Agent

Users must manually:

- Watch the full video
- Find highlights
- Cut short clips
- Write subtitles
- Add B-roll
- Add sound effects
- Add background music
- Adjust pacing
- Render videos

This workflow can take hours.

---

## After AI Clip Agent

Users only need to:

1. Upload video
2. Choose style
3. Wait for AI production
4. Review generated clips
5. Export final videos

---

## Target Outcome

From one raw video, the user should receive multiple short-form videos ready for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and other platforms.

---

## Differentiation

AI Clip Agent is not a traditional video editor.

It is an AI production system.

The difference is:

- Traditional editor: user edits manually
- AI Clip Agent: AI edits first, user reviews after

---

## Key Benefits

### Save Time

Reduce editing from hours to minutes.

### Produce More Content

Turn one long video into many short clips.

### Reduce Manual Work

AI handles repetitive editing tasks.

### Improve Consistency

Styles make every video follow a repeatable editing recipe.

### Speed Up Publishing

Creators can publish more frequently with less effort.
