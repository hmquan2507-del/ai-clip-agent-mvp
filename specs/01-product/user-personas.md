## Purpose

Define the primary user groups for AI Clip Agent.

These personas guide product decisions, workflow design, UI priorities, pricing, and AI pipeline behavior.

---

## Primary Persona 1: Solo Content Creator

### Description

A creator who records long-form videos, podcasts, livestreams, interviews, or educational content and wants to repurpose them into short-form videos.

### Main Goal

Create more short videos without spending hours editing.

### Pain Points

- Editing takes too much time
- Hard to find good highlights
- Subtitle editing is repetitive
- Adding B-roll and music is slow
- Posting consistently is difficult

### Desired Outcome

Upload one long video and receive multiple ready-to-review short videos.

---

## Primary Persona 2: Business Owner / Personal Brand

### Description

A business owner, coach, consultant, or expert who records talking-head content but does not want to manually edit videos.

### Main Goal

Turn expertise into social content quickly.

### Pain Points

- No time to edit
- Does not know what clips are interesting
- Needs professional-looking videos
- Wants consistent branding

### Desired Outcome

AI produces polished talking-head clips with subtitles, hooks, B-roll, and CTA.

---

## Primary Persona 3: Marketing Team

### Description

A small team that produces content for campaigns, brands, products, courses, or social channels.

### Main Goal

Increase content output without increasing editing workload.

### Pain Points

- Too many videos to process
- Manual editing creates bottlenecks
- Need consistent style across content
- Need fast turnaround

### Desired Outcome

Batch upload videos and get consistent short-form outputs for review and publishing.

---

## Primary Persona 4: Agency

### Description

A content or marketing agency managing multiple clients and producing short-form content at scale.

### Main Goal

Deliver more edited videos to clients faster.

### Pain Points

- Editing cost is high
- Client revisions take time
- Need repeatable production style
- Need organized review and export process

### Desired Outcome

Use AI Clip Agent as a production assistant for multiple client workspaces.

---

## Non-Primary Users

AI Clip Agent is not primarily built for:

- Professional editors who want full manual control
- Film editors
- Motion graphics specialists
- Users who want a complex timeline editor
- Users who only need basic trimming

---

## Persona Priority For MVP

MVP should focus on:

1. Solo Content Creator
2. Business Owner / Personal Brand
3. Small Marketing Team

Agency and enterprise workflows can come later.
